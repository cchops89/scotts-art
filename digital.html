Art that lives forever — on Bitcoin

Encouraged by his son Chris, Scott is now inscribing select paintings permanently onto Bitcoins as Ordinals. Physical painting and digital inscription — sold together.

Scott is partnering with the Bitcoin Museum in Nashville through @SatoshiHouseArt and @BMAG_HQ to bring fine botanical art to the Bitcoin community.
